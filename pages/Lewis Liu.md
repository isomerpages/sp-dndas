<h3><strong>Founder and Director of Crowdsense</strong></h3>
<p><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/lewis-liu-61408463/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Lewis</a> is
the Founder and Director of Crowdsense, a consultancy that helps organisations
engage stakeholders, identify emerging trends, and develop strategies for
impact. Over the past decade, he has worked across the public (PMO, MCCY),
private (Grab), and people sectors (<a href="http://better.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">better.sg</a>).</p>
<p>He began his career in the Public Service, where he supported agencies
in stakeholder engagement and policy feedback. He has designed and facilitated
workshops, focus groups, and experiential tours—including for the 21.5km
North-South Corridor—to surface ideas from diverse communities. He also
led a network of 200 public officer facilitators, equipping them with the
skills to handle complex dialogues.</p>
<p>Beyond his work at Crowdsense, Lewis is an Executive Education Fellow
at the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy (LKYSPP). Trained in design
thinking, ethnographic research, improv, and forum theatre, he brings both
rigour and creativity to his design and facilitation practice.</p>
