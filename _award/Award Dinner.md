<p>Join us for the <strong>Don Norman Design Award (DNDA) Award Ceremony &amp; Dinner</strong>,
an unforgettable evening celebrating innovation, design, and cultural diversity.
The event will honour outstanding early-career practitioners and educational
institutions who have made impactful contributions to <strong>Humanity-Centred Design (HCD+)</strong>.</p>
<p>The evening will feature a prestigious <strong>award ceremony</strong> where
we recognise the efforts of those shaping a more sustainable and equitable
future through design. Following the ceremony, enjoy a delightful <strong>dinner</strong> that
brings together design professionals, educators, and changemakers, fostering
networking and collaboration in an inspiring atmosphere.</p>
<p>To highlight Singapore’s rich cultural heritage, the dinner will feature
captivating <strong>performances</strong> that blend local traditions and
modern creativity, offering a unique experience that celebrates the fusion
of cultures in design and innovation.</p>
<p>This event is an opportunity to connect, engage, and celebrate the power
of design in shaping a better future. <strong>Register now</strong> to secure
your place at this special event and be part of an evening that honours
both design excellence and Singapore’s vibrant multicultural spirit.</p>
<p>We look forward to having you with us for this extraordinary celebration!</p>
